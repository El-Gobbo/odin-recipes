Sausage Ragu

Description

This is just a nice, simple recipe for sausage ragu. The fennel seeds really add a nice flavour to it.

Ingredients

  • 4 pork sausages, skins removed
  • 1 clove garlic, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp fennel seeds, ground
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • Pinch chilli flakes
  • 400g tinned tomatoes
  • Splash red wine (optional)
  • Pasta of choice

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Pinch pieces of sausage into the pan, then cook until browned.
  3. Add garlic, and cook for a minute.
  4. Add the rest of the spices, and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the tomatoes and wine, then simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. While the sauce is simmering, cook the pasta.
  7. Toss the cooked pasta with the sauce.
