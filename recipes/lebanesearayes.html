Lebanese Arayes

Description

Lebanese spiced lamb, cooked in pita bread. The key with this recipe is to cook the lamb inside the pita, so the juices leak into the bread as it cooks. It comes with a yoghurt and tahini dip, which adds real depth to the flavour of the meat. This version is made with an air fryer

Ingredients

  • 6 pita bread
  • Olive oil

Spiced Meat Filling

  • 1/2 brown onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 3/4 tsp ground allspice
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 1/4 cooking salt

Whipped Tahini Sauce

  • 1 cup plain yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/2 tsp cooking salt

Instructions

Spiced Meat Filling

  1. Grate the onion into a bowl, juices and all.
  2. Add remaining meat filling ingredients.
  3. Divide stuffing into 12 portions.
  4. Gently open a pita and place the meat inside. Push a small amount to the bottom of the pocket, then stuff the rest in. If the pita will not open, microwave it on full for 15 seconds.

Cooking

  1. Rub olive oil into both sides of the pita.
  2. Put them in the air fryer at 170C, for 4 mins on each side.
  3. Serve with whipped tahini sauce.

Whipped Tahini Sauce

  1. Place ingredients in a heatproof bowl and whisk to combine.
  2. Microwave for 15 seconds on high.
  3. Whisk again, then serve.
